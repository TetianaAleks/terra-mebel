Eco-style looks great in urban apartments, hotels, hostels, cafes, offices, and creative spaces. Furniture easily blends with loft, Scandinavian, nautical, tropical, ethnic, high-tech, or minimalist styles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your furniture made of?

Our furniture is made only from eco-friendly materials: Angara pine, birch, and beech. It does not contain MDF or pressed boards with toxic glue, which makes all our tables and chairs safe for children and pets.

Can I order something other than tables and chairs?

Yes, we also offer other types of furniture and accessories by custom order, including shelves, cabinets, side tables, and much more. You can discuss your requirements with us, and we will do our best to create the perfect piece of furniture for your interior.

Where do you deliver furniture?

We deliver furniture across Europe. We work with reliable transport companies to ensure safe and timely delivery. For specific delivery information to your country, please contact us.

Can I return the product if I don't like it?

We care about our customers, so we offer a return option within 14 days if the product doesn't meet your expectations or if you simply don't like it. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Please contact us for further details on the return process.

Do you assist with assembly?

Yes, we offer furniture assembly services. Our specialists will come and assemble your order at a time that is convenient for you. The assembly cost will be clarified when placing your order. Please contact us for more information.

Can I use self-pickup?

Yes, you can choose self-pickup. Once your order is ready, we will contact you to arrange the time and place for pickup. Please confirm the details with our manager when placing your order.
